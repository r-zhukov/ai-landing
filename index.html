Features

Create viral, human-like AI videos with these powerful tools
AI Assistant
Meet your creative partner. The AI Assistant helps you brainstorm, script, and refine video content with natural, conversational intelligence. It adapts to your style, suggests improvements, and accelerates your workflow so you can focus on what matters most: your message.
Content Vault
All your video assets, perfectly organized and instantly accessible. The Content Vault is your secure, searchable home for every draft, final cut, and creative resource. Never lose track of a file or waste time searching again.
Views Heatmap
See exactly where your audience is most engaged. The Views Heatmap visualizes attention, drop-off, and sharing points, helping you optimize every second of your video for maximum impact.
Workflows
Automate the tedious. Workflows let you set up publishing, collaboration, and approval pipelines that run themselves. Focus on creativity while the platform handles the busywork.
Vibe Marketing
Reach your audience everywhere. Vibe Marketing distributes your videos to every major platform, automatically optimizing formats and timing for maximum reach and engagement.
Smart Editing
Edit smarter, not harder. Instantly generate drafts, clone voices, and get AI-powered suggestions as you work. Smart Editing makes professional-quality video creation effortless.
